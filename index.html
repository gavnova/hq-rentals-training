💡 Pro Tips for New Staff
Things experienced staff wish they knew sooner
Vehicle Class vs Vehicle: Think of a Class as a "model" (e.g. Toyota Camry 2022 Blue) and a Vehicle as the actual physical car. Each car gets its own record.
Set to Reserved first: When adding a new car, always set status to "Reserved" until fully ready — prevents accidental online bookings.
Can't find an answer? Use the support ticket system inside the Knowledge Base — it goes directly to the HQ Rentals team.
